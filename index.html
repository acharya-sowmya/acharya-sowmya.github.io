About Me

I am a Graduate Research Assistant at University of Wisconsin-Madison in the Power Systems Engineering Research Centre (PSERC). I work with Prof. Christopher L. DeMarco on efficient compression of large datasets obtained from phasor measurement units.

My research interests include:

  • Power system operations and control
  • Data analytics
  • Wide-area Measurement Systems
  • Optimization
